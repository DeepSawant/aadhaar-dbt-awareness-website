Enable DBT on Your Bank Account

Follow these steps to seed your Aadhaar and set your primary DBT account in the NPCI mapper.

Prerequisites

  • Your 12-digit Aadhaar number
  • A bank account you wish to receive benefits in
  • Registered mobile number with active SMS
  • Valid KYC documents (PAN, address)

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Seed Aadhaar with Your Bank

    Visit your bank branch, internet banking, or mobile app to seed Aadhaar with your chosen account. Some banks allow Aadhaar seeding via ATM.

  2. Set as Primary DBT Account

    Ask the bank to mark this account as your primary DBT account in the NPCI mapper. This ensures benefits are credited here.

  3. Verify Mapping

    Within a few days, confirm with your bank channels that your Aadhaar is seeded and DBT is enabled.

  4. Troubleshoot Issues

    If credits fail or go to a different account, request your bank to update the NPCI mapper to the correct account.
