🔍 Check Your Aadhaar DBT Status

Verify if your Aadhaar is properly linked and DBT-enabled with your bank account. Get instant guidance on your next steps.

What we check:

  • ✓ If your Aadhaar is linked to your bank account
  • ✓ If DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) is enabled
  • ✓ If your account is set as primary for receiving benefits
  • ✓ Any potential issues that might block transfers

Your data is safe:

  • 🔒 We don't store your personal information
  • 🔒 All checks are done locally in your browser
  • 🔒 No data is sent to external servers
  • 🔒 This is for educational purposes only
Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number (spaces will be added automatically)
10-digit mobile number registered with your bank and Aadhaar
For verification purposes only - we only need last 4 digits

What happens after checking?

✅

Fully Enabled

Your account is properly linked and DBT-enabled. You should receive benefits without issues.

Next step: Monitor your benefit payments and keep KYC updated.
⚠️

Partially Set Up

Your Aadhaar is linked but DBT might not be fully enabled for all benefits.

Next step: Visit your bank to complete DBT enabling process.
❌

Not Linked

Your account is not properly set up for receiving government benefits.

Next step: Follow our step-by-step guide to set it up.
