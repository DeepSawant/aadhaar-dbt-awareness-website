    Helpful Documents & Guides

    Step-by-step Guide: Enabling DBT
    1. Visit your bank: Go to the branch where you hold your account
    2. Carry documents: Aadhaar card, account details, identity proof
    3. Fill forms: Complete Aadhaar seeding and DBT enabling forms
    4. Verification: Bank staff will verify your documents
    5. Wait period: Allow 5-10 working days for processing
    6. Follow up: Check status through bank channels after the wait period
    Documents Checklist

    Always carry:

    • Original Aadhaar card (or certified copy)
    • Bank passbook or account statement
    • One additional photo ID (PAN, passport, voter ID)
    • Mobile number (same as linked with Aadhaar)

    May be required:

    • Recent passport-size photographs
    • Address proof (if different from Aadhaar)
    • Income certificate (for certain schemes)
    Troubleshooting Common Issues

    If Aadhaar linking fails:

    • Ensure name spelling matches exactly across documents
    • Update mobile number if changed since Aadhaar enrollment
    • Check if Aadhaar is active (not suspended)

    If DBT is not working:

    • Confirm account is active and not frozen
    • Verify correct account number with benefit-issuing agency
    • Ensure only one account is set as primary for DBT
