Quick Facts

What's the difference? Aadhaar-linking lets your bank identify you. DBT-enabling designates your account to receive government benefits.
How long does it take? Usually 5-10 working days after visiting your bank to complete the DBT enabling process.
Is there any cost? No! Banks provide Aadhaar seeding and DBT enabling services free of charge.
